FAQ

Keep it simple, honest, and confidence-building (perfect for a prototype).

How often do burrs need sharpening?

It depends on usage, beans, and burr material. For home users, it’s typically a long-interval service — which is why a website MVP is ideal.

Will sharpening change burr geometry?

The goal is to restore cutting edges without changing the intended burr design. If we spot damage or heavy wear, we’ll flag it in the report.

What grinders do you support?

Most home grinders with removable burr sets. In the prototype, you can list common brands in the booking form dropdown.

What if my burrs are too worn?

We’ll notify you before proceeding and recommend replacement if sharpening wouldn’t deliver a meaningful improvement.
